Cancellation and Refund Policy

1. Subscription Plan Cancellation

Landlord Plan Subscriptions: Landlords may cancel their subscription Plan at any time before the renewal date. Once canceled, the subscription will not be renewed for the following month, but no refund will be provided for the current billing cycle.

2. Payment Refunds

Failed Transactions: In the case of failed transactions where the payment is deducted but the service is not rendered, RentⓝMeter.Receipt will investigate the issue. If the error is on our end, a full refund will be provided within 7-10 business days.

Duplicate Payments: If a payment is made more than once for the same service, the duplicate amount will be refunded within 7-10 business days.

3. Cancellation of Service

Landlord-Initiated Cancellations: Landlords may cancel the service at any time. Upon cancellation, access to RentⓝMeter.Receipt services will be terminated at the end of the current billing cycle. No refunds will be issued for the remaining period.

Tenant-Initiated Cancellations: Tenants who wish to cancel their rental payments must notify their landlord directly. RentⓝMeter.Receipt is not responsible for processing cancellations initiated by tenants.

4. Refund Process

Requesting a Refund: To request a refund, users must contact RentⓝMeter.Receipt customer support at rnm.receipt@gmail.com with details of the transaction and the reason for the refund request.

Processing Time: Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days after the refund request has been approved.

Refund Method: Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the transaction. If this is not possible, an alternative method will be arranged with the user.

5. Exceptions

Non-Refundable Services: Certain services, such as one-time setup fees, custom development, and promotional discounts, are non-refundable. These will be clearly marked at the time of purchase.

Force Majeure: RentⓝMeter.Receipt is not liable for any delays or failures in processing refunds due to circumstances beyond our control, including but not limited to natural disasters, acts of war, or other unforeseen events.

6. Amendments to the Policy

Changes to the Policy: RentⓝMeter.Receipt reserves the right to amend this Cancellation and Refund Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting on our website. Users are encouraged to review this policy periodically.
