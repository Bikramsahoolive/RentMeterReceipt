Rent Bill User Guide

Create Rent Bill

Select Rentholder Name:

Choose the rentholder from whom the bill will be created.

Monthly Rent Status:

Select the monthly rent status.

Electric Bill Status:

Choose the electric bill status from the following options:

Paid:

Electric bill not applicable, amount will be 0.

Create Electric Bill by Main Meter Bill Details:

Fill in the following:

  • Main meter bill total amount.
  • Main meter bill total unit consumed.
  • Previous unit of sub meter (auto-filled but can be modified).
  • Current unit of sub meter.

Create Electric Bill by Per Unit Amount:

Fill in the following:

  • Previous unit of sub meter (auto-filled but can be modified).
  • Current unit of sub meter.
  • Per unit amount.

Create Electric Bill by Amount:

Simply fill in the electric bill amount directly.

Adjust Unit:

This field is associated with Create Electric Bill by Main Meter Bill Details and Create Electric Bill by Per Unit Amount . It is optional and can be filled if the landlord needs to increase or decrease the unit consumption of the rentholder. It accepts both positive and negative values.

Additional Optional Fields:

  • Water Bill Amount
  • Previous Due Amount: This field also accepts both positive and negative values.

Submit and Print:

Submit the form and the bill will be ready to print.
