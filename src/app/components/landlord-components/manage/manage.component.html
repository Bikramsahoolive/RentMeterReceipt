User Guide

Add Rentholder

Name of the Rentholder:

Fill in the name of the rentholder in the name field.

Member Count:

Fill in the number of persons who will stay in the rental property in the member count field.

Contact Information:

Fill in the phone number and email of the rentholder.

Monthly Rent:

Fill in the monthly rent of the rentholder.

Initial Sub Meter Unit (Optional):

If the rental property has a sub meter, fill in the current sub meter unit at the time of registration (optional).

Rentholder Photo (Optional):

Choose a photo of the rentholder (optional).

Rentholder Documents:

Choose rentholder documents like ID card or/and rent agreement.

Password:

Enter a password between 8 to 16 digits and confirm the password.

Submit:

Submit the form to create the rentholder profile.

Bill Payment (Offline)

Bill ID:

The landlord has to enter the bill ID and get the bill amount automatically.

Modify Bill Payment Amount:

The landlord can modify the bill payment amount for partial payments (minimum 1 to maximum pending bill amount).

Payment:

Submit the paid amount to generate receipt.
